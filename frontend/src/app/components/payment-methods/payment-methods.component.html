Frequently Asked Questions

When will my payment be processed?

Mobile money payments are processed instantly. Card payments are instant but may take 1-3 business days to show in your bank statement. Bank transfers take 1-3 business days to process.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, all fees are clearly displayed. The only costs are the processing fees shown for each payment method. Your bank may charge additional fees for international transactions.

What if my payment fails?

If your payment fails, please try again or contact our support team. No charges will be applied for failed transactions. We recommend checking with your bank if card payments fail.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are processed according to our refund policy. Generally, refunds are available up to 30 days before the event date. Processing fees may not be refundable.
