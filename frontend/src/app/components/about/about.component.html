About Mwl.Nyerere International Marathon

Celebrating Tanzania's premier marathon event that brings together runners from across the globe

flag Our Mission

The Mwl.Nyerere International Marathon is dedicated to promoting health, unity, and community spirit through the power of running. Named after Tanzania's founding father, Mwalimu Julius Nyerere, this marathon embodies his vision of unity (Ujamaa) and brings together people from all walks of life to celebrate endurance, determination, and the beautiful spirit of Tanzania.

Marathon Overview

history Our History

Established in 2024, the Mwl.Nyerere International Marathon has grown from a local community event to one of East Africa's most prestigious running competitions. Each year, we welcome thousands of participants from over 40 countries, creating a truly international celebration of running.

  • Founded in 2024
  • Over 40 countries represented
  • 3,000+ annual participants
  • IAAF certified course
location_on The Route

Our marathon course takes runners through the heart of Dar es Salaam, showcasing the city's vibrant culture, historic landmarks, and stunning coastal views. The route has been carefully designed to provide both challenge and beauty, with enthusiastic crowd support throughout.

  • Scenic coastal route
  • Historic city center
  • 15 medical stations
  • Enthusiastic local support
